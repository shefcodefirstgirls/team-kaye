Singapore
Traveller : Kat

I was lucky enough to live in Singapore for 2 years doing research! Plenty of time to explore and compile the ultimate ‘best of’ list.

1. Gardens by the Bay

Travel the world in two large greenhouses, including the largest indoor waterfall! Then feel like you’ve gone forward in time as you witness the super trees light up in time to music.

- Garden rhapsody: Every day at 7:45pm and 8:45pm
- Nearest metro: Bayfront
- Nearest food court: Satay by the bay

2. Walk around Marina Bay

Completely reclaimed from the sea, this 4km walk showcases the best views of Singapores urban skyline

- Best view: Helix bridge
- Nearest metro: Marina Bay
- Nearest food court: Rasapura masters in Marina Bay Sands
- Nearest bar: Kinki, happy hour 5-8pm

3. Treetop walk at MacRitchie Reservoir

Forget the city life and walk amongst the tree tops. Watch out for monkeys!

- Nearest MRT: Marymount
- Bring your own food! Perfect for picnics

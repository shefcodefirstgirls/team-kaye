Traveller: Kaylee

Hi guys! I got the chance to visit Switzerland in September 2017! We flew into Geneva Airport, Switzerland, from Manchester, England, and from there hired a car before setting off! The primary aim for the visit was to conduct some fieldwork for the University fo Sheffield Geography Department and as part of a GLOSS Research Associate scheme I was a part of at the time.

Our first destination was Oberwald, a small village located in Obergoms located within the canton of Valais. This si a beautiful village full of traditional Swiss housing. One house even had cows in a pasture at thr front fo the house!
